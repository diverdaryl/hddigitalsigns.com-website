History of Out-of-Home Advertising

Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising has been part of human communication for centuries. It began with ancient wall paintings and public signage, evolved into printed posters in the 1800s, and became iconic large-scale billboards in the 20th century. Today, OOH is revolutionized by LED and digital screens, blending creativity and technology to engage audiences everywhere.

OOH Trivia

Fun and surprising facts about the world of Out-of-Home advertising!

🪧 The First Billboard
